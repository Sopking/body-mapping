About this exercise: Body mapping is a simple way for you to report any aches, pains or discomfort in your back, joints or muscles that may be caused or made worse by your work. Your responses are treated confidentially — they will be used only to identify common patterns and inform health & safety improvements. Pain should not be part of your job.
